## 🎯 IV. Phase 1: Proof of Concept (POC) Goals

The objective of Phase 1 is to demonstrate the **Technical Viability** of the Aegis-X architecture using a Tier-1 Sovereign Prototype. Success in this phase triggers the "Greenlight" for tactical field expansion.

### **1. The "Clean Spectrum" Milestone**
* **Goal:** Achieve a 99% accuracy rate in distinguishing between benign civilian RF traffic (WiFi/Bluetooth) and simulated "Threat Signatures" from the `threat_signatures.json` library.
* **Success Metric:** Zero false-positive alerts during a 24-hour continuous monitoring cycle in a high-density signal environment (St. Pete, FL).

### **2. Forensic Integrity Verification**
* **Goal:** Successfully pass a manual "Red Team" audit of the `Aegis_BlackBox.log`.
* **Success Metric:** The `aegis_validator.py` script must detect 100% of attempted manual alterations to the log file, proving the SHA-256 chain is un-breakable.

### **3. Latency & Logic Saturation Test**
* **Goal:** Measure the time from "Signal Detection" to "Resonant Pulse Recommendation" by the Llama 3.1 AI Bridge.
* **Success Metric:** Sub-500ms latency on local M2 hardware. In the world of electronic warfare, speed is the only armor.

### **4. Auditory Multi-Tasking Readiness**
* **Goal:** Verify that the "Commander Daniel" voice-over module provides 100% situational awareness without requiring the operator to view the terminal.
* **Success Metric:** Successful "Blind Identifications" of 10 different random signal injections using only auditory cues.
