## 🚛 II. Distribution & Logistics Costs

The distribution of a Sovereign Shield requires **Chain of Custody** integrity to ensure no units are intercepted or tampered with prior to deployment.

### **1. Secure Fulfillment (Domestic)**
* **Bonded Courier Shipping:** To maintain security, units are not shipped via standard postal services. Secured logistics (FedEx Custom Critical or similar) is required.
* **Estimated Cost:** **$150 - $400** per unit.

### **2. Compliance & Export Controls (ITAR)**
* **Legal Shielding:** Legal council to navigate ITAR and FCC part 15/90 compliance for domestic defense use.
* **Estimated Setup:** **$7,500 - $15,000** (Initial Flat Fee).

### **3. Operational Support (The "AI Bridge")**
* **Maintenance:** Periodic updates to the `threat_signatures.json` library.
* **Cost:** **$0** (Decentralized/Sovereign model).
